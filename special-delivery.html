First Slavic Evangelical Baptist Church Presents

Special Delivery

A Christmas Musical Experience

On a quiet night in Bethlehem, Heaven’s greatest gift arrived in the humblest of places. Tonight, through music, story, and light, we remember the King who was wrapped not in robes, but in swaddling cloths.

Join us as angels announce, shepherds marvel, and the world receives a Special Delivery.

After the Musical

The Glowing Courtyard

Stay, linger, and enjoy fellowship under the Christmas lights.

After the final note, you’re warmly invited to step outside and continue the celebration. We’ve prepared a cozy courtyard where you can savor the beauty of the season together.

Fire & Warmth

Gather around the outdoor fireplaces and take in the crisp winter air. Share stories, laugh together, and enjoy the glow of the evening.

Hot Chocolate & Café

Visit our café area for complimentary hot chocolate, warm drinks, and seasonal treats prepared with love. A warm cup in your hands, and joy in your heart.

Lights & Displays

Walk through the Christmas light displays designed to reflect the wonder of the nativity. Take a quiet moment to reflect, or simply enjoy the beauty.

Photos & Activities

Capture the evening at our photo spots and enjoy activities and crafts for families and children. Make memories that will stay with you long after tonight.

Blessing

Light for the Journey

May the Light of Christ, born in Bethlehem, fill your heart with peace, your home with joy, and your life with hope this Christmas season.

Thank you for joining us for Special Delivery. Merry Christmas from the Special Delivery Musical Team.
